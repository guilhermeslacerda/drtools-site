How to cite? /

To cite DR-Tools Suite, use the following bibtex entry: 
                
                    @manual{drtools:2023,
                        title={DR-Tools Suite},
                        author={Guilherme Lacerda},
                        year={2023},
                        note={available in http://drtools.site/}
                    }
